How to Obtain Your National ID Card

  1. 1

    Book an Appointment

    Use our online system to schedule an appointment at your convenience.

  2. 2

    Prepare Documents

    Gather required documents: Birth Certificate, Proof of Residence, Previous ID (if applicable), and Passport Photos.

  3. 3

    Visit Identification Office

    Attend your appointment and submit your documents.

  4. 4

    Biometric Capture

    Complete the biometric data capture process.
